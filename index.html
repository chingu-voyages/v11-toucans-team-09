Our Chef

Michelle is our head chef at Signature 21. After graduating from the Culinary Institute of America where she earned a degree in Culinary Arts, Michelle spent time cooking in various restaurants in New York. She was hired as an assistant to the chef Scott’s flagship restaurant where she was mentored under the great chef Scott.

Michelle joined the team at Signature21’s launch as head chef creating a menu of excellent cuisine that is enjoyed by people from around the world. At the helm of a restaurant her cuisine has been awarded with various rave reviews by many food critics.

Our Story

We always believed that as long as we put all of our energy into creating a flawless dining experience for our customers, in time we would be known for just that. We have set out to create a modern and eclectic dining experience with our cuisine combined with the backdrop of an inviting and elegantly designed dining room.
